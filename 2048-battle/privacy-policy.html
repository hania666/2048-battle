2048 Battle - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

2048 Battle collects minimal information to provide our services: nickname, device ID, game statistics, and app settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to enable multiplayer matchmaking, display leaderboards, save your game progress, and improve game performance.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud services. Local data is stored on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Advertising

2048 Battle uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

5. Children Privacy

2048 Battle is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@2048battle.app. You can also clear local data by uninstalling the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@2048battle.app
